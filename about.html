I like to think of myself as a creative problem solver.

I am a visual designer, marketer and a coder by night. I also have an academic background in International Relations and Geography. In my spare time you’ll find me café hopping, reading and sketching my surroundings.

My passion for design started when I was 12 years old, curiously experimenting with Illustrator and Photoshop on my family’s Macintosh. After learning from mentors along the way, I began designing professionally since 2014 and coding since 2017.

This website is my home for documenting client work and personal work throughout my life.
