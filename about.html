I think of myself as a creative problem solver.

My name is Kyle. I am a one of a kind marketer who designs and codes. I also have an academic background in International Relations and Geography. In my spare time you’ll find me café hopping, reading critical theory books and taking photos.

My passion for design started when I was 12 years old, curiously experimenting with Illustrator and Photoshop on my family’s Macintosh. After learning from mentors along the way, I began designing professionally since 2014 and coding since 2017.

This website is my home for documenting client work and personal work throughout my life.

Message me at kylewag@me.com — my inbox is always open. If you’re in Southeast Asia, stop by Luang Prabang and have a drink. Otherwise, stalk me on social media below.
