Background
A marketing campaign was built around two microsites for a local DMC to provide a quick method for booking tours.
Target Audience
Travellers 25-30 years old living in large Asian cities who are seeking a short break of 2-3 days. These people are looking for nature, culture and adventure. They are middle-class, impatient, crave selfies, need flexibility and are tech-savvy. We chose Singapore as our target city.
Challenge
Luang Prabang has strong cultural traditions and scenic landscapes, yet it is still relatively unknown. Local DMCs lack online booking engines and use e-mail to confirm bookings.
Solution
The goal is to create a ROI-driven social media campaign to promote special tour packages with a faster booking solution.
