Background
Luang Prabang is a relatively unknown and untapped destination. Travel agencies lack online tools and are slow to confirm bookings. The goal was a ROI-driven social media campaign to book tour packages quickly on a microsite.
Target Audience
Travellers 25-30 years old living in large Asian cities who are seeking a short break of 2-3 days. These people are looking for nature, culture and adventure. They are middle-class, impatient, crave selfies, need flexibility and are tech-savvy. We chose Singapore as our target city.
Advertisements
We told a story through a carousel of 4 images in Facebook and Instagram ads.
Scoot Opportunity
Scoot was launching direct flights to Luang Prabang during the campaign, so we developed an advertisement and integrated this promotion into the microsite.
Testimonial Ads
The second phase of our campaign was a carousel of testimonials written by actual Singaporeans.
Microsite
We launched two mobile microsites for the 2-day and 3-day tours.
