Background
Luang Prabang is a relatively unknown and untapped destination. Travel agencies lack online tools and are slow to confirm bookings. The goal was a ROI-driven social media campaign to book tour packages quickly on a microsite.
Target Audience
We targeted travelers 25-30 years old in Singapore who seek a 2-3 day tour with nature, culture and adventure. They’re middle-class, impatient, crave selfies, need flexibility and are tech-savvy.
Advertisements
We told a story through a carousel of 4 images in Facebook and Instagram ads.
