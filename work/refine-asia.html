Refine Asia Identity Development
Branding & Identity
Approach
This rebranding refreshes Refine Asia’s identity, elevating its style and sophistication to the level of a revered luxury brand. We avoid looking materially kitsch, and instead focus on delivering a high quality travel experiences to clients with an understated brand appearance.
Brand Idea
Our icon symbolizes an ancient coin and connects back to the brand name: Refine Asia. It is refined, polished and reflects a former time of wealth.
Logotype
The complete Refine Asia logotype consists of both the icon and the wordmark together. The primary version of the logotype is uses our dark blue background.
Colors
The two primary colours are midnight blue and Buddha gold. These colours represent the strong identity of Refine Asia, giving it its classy look. The off-white Coconut Milk colour is used for the typography and as backgrounds to give it a softer, warm feel. Coconut Milk and Buddha gold strongly tie in with colours found throughout Southeast Asia.
Typography
Our typeface for our brand and logotype is Chap, released in 2016 by Lauri Toikka & Florian Schick. Chap is a mix of the smooth and the sharp, and is a modern typeface that pays tribute to sans-serif while retaining small elements of calligraphy.
Application
Refine Asia's logo is applied to a fictional campaign to simulate a real-world scenario.
Website
A fully functional website was built using Boostrap 4, AOS, Fontawesome, and other javascript libraries.
